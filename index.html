Twenty years of turning maps into memories.

We started Junior Tours because too many trips felt like a checklist: same bus, same photo stop, same rushed lunch. Ours run smaller and slower. Most groups top out at fourteen people, guided by someone who has walked the route a hundred times and still points things out you'd have missed.

Some travelers want a set departure with a group. Others want a private itinerary built around three weeks off work and a very specific idea of a good time. We plan both the same way: carefully, and with a person on the other end of the phone who actually answers.
